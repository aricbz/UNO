192 Cards 2–6 Players Age 7+ + Expansion Pack

🎯Objective Be the first to empty your hand — or knock all other players out of the game by giving them 25+ cards.
Setup
1

Choose a dealer and shuffle the deck. Tip: split between multiple players — it's a big deck.

2

Deal 7 cards to each player.

3

Place remaining cards face-down in the center. This is the Draw Pile.

4

Flip the top card face-up to start the Discard Pile. If it's an Action Card, ignore it and flip the next.

5

Player to the left of the dealer goes first. Play moves clockwise.

On Your Turn
✓
You HAVE a matching card
Match color, number, or symbol
›
Play one card onto the Discard Pile. It must match at least one attribute of the top card: its color, number, or symbol. If it's an Action Card, its effect triggers immediately.
✕
You DON'T have a match
Keep drawing until you can play
›
Draw cards from the Draw Pile one at a time until you draw a card you can play. Then play that card immediately.
Calling "UNO!"
⚠️
When you're down to 1 card, you must yell "UNO!" If someone catches you before the next player's turn begins, you draw 2 cards.
Special Rules

Stacking

If a Draw Card (+2, +4, +6, +10) is played on you, you can counter with a Draw Card of equal or higher value. The penalty stacks until someone can't respond — they draw the full total.

7's Swap

When you play a 7, you must swap your entire hand with any player of your choice.

0's Pass

When you play a 0, ALL players pass their hand to the next player in the current direction of play.

Winning
🏆
Play your last card to win the hand — or knock out all other players to be the last one standing.
