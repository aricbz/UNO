Dare System
In a Nutshell

UNO Dare plays like regular UNO — except when you can't play a card, other players can force you to choose: draw cards or perform a dare. Dares range from easy to outright embarrassing. You won't know the level until you roll the die.

Objective
🎯
Be the first player to get rid of all cards in your hand — or avoid being the last one standing.
Setting Up
  1. 1
    Choose a dealer and shuffle all cards thoroughly.
  2. 2
    Deal 7 cards face-down to each player.
  3. 3
    Place remaining cards face-down in the center — this is the Draw Pile.
  4. 4
    Flip the top card of the Draw Pile face-up to start the Discard Pile. If it's an Action Card, ignore it and flip the next card.
  5. 5
    The player to the left of the dealer goes first. Play proceeds clockwise.
💡
Keep the Dare List Cards somewhere everyone can reach them — you'll need them each time a dare is rolled.
Your Turn
🃏
On your turn, play one card onto the Discard Pile. A card is playable if it matches the color, number, or symbol of the top card.
Have a match?

Play it on the Discard Pile. If it's an Action Card, something happens!

No match?

Draw one card. If it's playable, you may play it immediately.

⚠️
You may also choose to draw even when you have a playable card in hand.
Calling UNO!
UNO!

When you have only 1 card left, you must immediately yell "UNO!" to warn other players.

If another player catches you before the next turn begins — you must draw 2 cards as penalty.

Winning
🏆
The first player to play their final card wins — or continue and have the last person standing take the L ... and hold it close.
Draw Pile Empty?
🔄
If the Draw Pile runs out, reshuffle the Discard Pile (leaving the top card in place) to form a new Draw Pile.

Dare Levels
🌶️
Mild
Easy warm-up dares
Level 1
The easiest tier. Designed to get everyone comfortable. Think: do a silly voice, tell a joke, do 10 jumping jacks. Fun without stakes.
🌶️🌶️
Medium
Awkward but doable
Level 2
Things start to get uncomfortable here. Embarrassing but not scandalous. Expect social awkwardness.
🔥
Spicy
Daring and more personal
Level 3
Getting into real dare territory. More personal, more daring, higher stakes social currency. Read the room before playing at this level.
💀
Fuego
No mercy — adults only
Level 4 — 18+
The hardest tier. Not for the faint of heart. Match the number on the Dare Card with the number on the corresponding Dare List Card to find your specific dare. All players must agree on whether the dare was successfully completed.
Completing A Dare
✅
Some dares are subjective. All players must agree whether it was completed. Strictness is up to your group.
Chickening Out
🐔
Accepted the dare, rolled the die, but now don't want to do it? You may back out — but you must draw 4 cards as penalty.
